2.Description of StreetSigns:

 

StreetSigns is a Signsource. It contains over 650 signs which are divided into 24 language categories. Each category is represented by an urban metaphor. Signs of Math and Calculations are in the Wall Street section, while signs of Inquiry may be found at the Newsstand.

 

The students provided the original artwork for each category. This artwork is as important to the context of each sign as the category name because the drawings place each sign in a visual environment which gives strong clues to the sign meaning.
