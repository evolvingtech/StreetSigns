The Virtual Alphabet Book is a project created by Susan Abdulezer to expand the experience of the traditional alphabet in an non-traditional way. Six modes of access for the alphabet provide a vehicle for any type of learner to experience, sequence, manipulate and explore each of the 26 letters. Hand Signs, Lip-Reading movies, 3-D Braille Dots, Alphabet Animations, Virtual Objects and Soundscapes have been created for each letter.
