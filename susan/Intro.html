1.Introduction:

StreetSigns:A City Kid's Guide to American Sign Language was developed in conjunction with the children of Junior High School 47, School for the Deaf, in New York City. The children contributed artwork, computer graphics, and their own unique take on urban life in New York.

The Signers featured in StreetSigns are all current or former students at the school. Elementary, Junior High School and Work Study students all participated.
