10. Virtual Letters and Objects:

 

In collaboration with young children we chose 26 items for the Virtual Objects alphabet. Click on a letter and an object will appear. You can then explore that object by rotating it with a cursor. Try out the Virtual Bear by clicking on the image above. To take advantage of this on the Web you will need to download the Quicktime plug-in.It will take about 5 minutes to download, so "bear" with it!

You can also organize letters on a screen by dragging them out of the LetterBox.

Or you can twirl a letter around and see it from all angles.
